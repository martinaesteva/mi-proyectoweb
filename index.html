About me

Hello! I’m Martina Esteva, a passionate audiovisual designer and illustrator currently in my fourth year of the Audiovisual Design and Illustration’s degree at UDIT Madrid.

Throughout my studies, I’ve explored various disciplines, from animation and graphic design to digital illustration. I love combining traditional and digital techniques to create projects that capture attention and evoke emotions.

During my academic formation, I’ve had the opportunity to work on several collaborative projects. I’m always open to new challenges and excited about continuing to learn and evolve in the design field.

In this portfolio, you’ll find a selection of my standout works. I hope you enjoy exploring my creations. Thank you for visiting!
